Import ETH Tokens
Using Ethplorer, the current balance of the tokens in your ETH wallet can be imported into Cryptofolio. Your Ethereum token holdings would either get added to your current holdings, or would replace them depending on which option you choose. Tokens that aren't listed on CoinGecko would not get added.
No-API Account
This progress bar displays how much storage space is left to store No-API mode data. Since the No-API mode isn't meant for long-term use, there are no protections against the loss of data. If there's no remaining storage, many of Cryptofolio's functions may cease to work right. In such an event, it's recommended to export your data to keep it safe.
